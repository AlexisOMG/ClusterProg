Preparatory part (10 min):
General developmental exercises (ORU):
I.P. - initial position; O.S. - main rack
1) I.P .: O.S. 1-arms to the sides; 2-hands up, stand up on socks; 3-arms to the sides; 4-I.P. (4 times);
2) I.P .: hands to the sides. 1-clap in front of you; 2-claps behind the back (4-5 times);
3) I.P .: feet shoulder-width apart, hands behind the back, fingers intertwined. 1-lean forward, swing hands backward; 2-I.P. (4-5 times);
4) I.P .: feet shoulder-width apart, hands on the belt. 1-turn of the body to the right; 2 - also in the opposite direction (5-6 times);
5) I.P .: hands on the belt, legs together. 1-jump, return to I.P .; 2-4, also with a left turn (4-5 times);
Main part (40 min):
• Squats 10 times (3 sets) (10 min);
• Lunges 10 times (3 sets) (10 min);
• Bicycle (2 sets) (5 min);
• Twisting with lifting upside down 25 times (3 sets) (8 min);
• Plank (1 min 30 sec)
• Twisting lying down 25 times (3 sets) (10 min)
• Stretching (10 min):
1. Tilt to the side (2 min);
2. Side exit (2 min);
3. Butterfly pose (3 min);
4. Bend forward (3 min).
Final part (10 min):
• Stretching for all muscle groups (10 min).
